Join Us

UterUs is more than just an organization; it's a movement dedicated to empowering menstruators and creating a more sustainable future. Founded by Imani Riddick-Cherry and Leah Beth Warren as their Honor’s project, UterUs has become a key player in changing the way we talk about menstruation, reproductive health, and environmental sustainability.

By joining UterUs, you become part of a community that’s committed to:

  • Breaking the Stigma: We engage in open and honest conversations about menstruation, helping to normalize discussions that are often overlooked or considered taboo.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Through our work with eco-friendly alternatives, such as reusable menstrual pads, we aim to reduce the environmental impact of traditional period products.
  • Accessibility: We ensure that all menstruators, regardless of their economic situation, have access to affordable, sustainable menstrual products. Our initiatives, such as donating menstrual cups and hosting fundraising events, help make this a reality.
  • Education and Advocacy: We provide resources to educate people on reproductive health, helping individuals take charge of their health and cycles in a safe and informed manner.
  • Making a Difference: Your participation supports our mission to empower individuals and promote environmental consciousness. Together, we can make lasting change.

UterUs is actively growing and organizing engaging events such as workshops, fundraisers, and fellowship gatherings to bring people together for a common cause. Whether you’re interested in attending our “Get A Clue” events, participating in our Earth Day celebrations, or engaging in monthly fellowships, there’s always something to do and someone to meet.

Join us today and become part of a movement that values sustainability, education, and advocacy for menstruators everywhere!
