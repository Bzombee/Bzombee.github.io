Mission Statement

  • Empower menstruators to explore safer, more environmentally sustainable, and less expensive alternatives to common period products.
  • Engage in open and honest conversations about menstruation.
  • Improve the way people perceive change within their menstrual cycles each month.
  • Educate users on reproductive health management

Goals

  • Increase environmental consciousness relating to menstrual health
  • Ensure accessibility to sustainable menstrual products to all menstruators in our community
