Site Map

Page Design

Home Page (index.html)

  • Purpose: Main landing page.
  • Audience: All visitors (members, prospective members, external organizations).
  • Content: Slideshow, social media links, navigation bar, recruitment section.
  • Interactivity: Slideshow animation, clickable recruitment CTA button.

About Page

  • Purpose: Provide background information on UterUS.
  • Audience: Potential members and interested parties.
  • Content: Organization history, mission statement, goals.

Recruitment Page

  • Purpose: Allow interested users to sign up.
  • Audience: Potential new members.
  • Content: Contact form (fields: Name, School Email, Referral Source, Questions).
  • Interactivity: Form submission via AJAX, validation for required fields.

Contact Page

  • Purpose: Provide direct contact information.
  • Audience: General inquiries from members and external organizations.
  • Content: Leadership team contact details, inquiry form.
  • Interactivity: Form submission via AJAX, email validation.

Events Page

  • Purpose: Showcase past and upcoming events.
  • Audience: Members and prospective members.
  • Content: Event descriptions, dates, images, calendar of upcoming events.
  • Interactivity: Clickable event details, filter by event type.

Officer Page

  • Purpose: Introduce club leadership.
  • Audience: Members and prospective members.
  • Content: Photos, names, bios, contact info.
